## Collection Method

Content was collected using:
- **YouTube transcripts:** Infocaptor public transcript database + video page metadata and chapters
- **Blog/newsletter content:** HTTP fetch from public URLs (Substack, Ahrefs blog, Clearscope blog)
- **LinkedIn posts:** Curated from public posts and cross-referenced blog/newsletter content
- **Video metadata:** YouTube page metadata and chapter timestamps

All content is organized by expert and source type in the subfolders of this `/research/` directory.
